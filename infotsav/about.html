INFOTSAV'20

ABOUT:

Infotsav is the techno-manegerial fest of Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, aimed at nurturing creativity and innovation among the youth in a competitive as well as a recreational manner. Participants hail from Premier technical and manegerial institutions of the region, and form a wide range of backgrounds.

A plethora of technical events like Hackathon , Techathlon , Robowars etc. , managerial events like Pinnacle , Brandsome , Sameeksha etc., and a lot of fun events will also take place during the course of the fest.
